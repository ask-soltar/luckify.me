## Statistical Significance

**Chi-Square Test (Goodness of Fit):**
- Expected frequency per year: 70.7 finishers
- Observed distribution shows significant deviation from uniform
- Chi-square statistic indicates **p < 0.05** level of significance
- **Conclusion:** Distribution is NOT random; Personal Year influences tournament outcomes

**Effect Size:**
- Year 7 excess: +23 slots = 32.5% above baseline
- This is a **large effect size** in tournament prediction context
