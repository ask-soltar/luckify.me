The Pursuer
Blue is the rhythm of curiosity in motion — capable of a fast rise to success and equally susceptible to great falls. The Pursuer is always moving toward something: an environment to read, a goal to chase, a puzzle to solve, a connection to make. The cycle is its defining architecture. Rise steeply, cup at the peak, then restart or crash. Blue does not operate in a straight line — it operates in cycles that may connect across time itself, picking up where a previous arc left off across sessions, days, or years.
◈
Dual-Source Architecture: Blue is the only rhythm channeled through two distinct collectives. The primary framework comes through the Zeta Collective — a scientific intelligence, which explains Blue's heavy I Ching Gate structure and precision mapping. The Consumed trait specifically attracts Lyran channeling through its Lake Energy mechanism, suggesting Lyrans carry a different intelligence — possibly more experiential or intuitive — that complements Zeta's analytical precision. The relationship between these two collectives within Blue is an open architectural question with system-wide implications.
Two-Layer Architecture
Birth Rhythm
A person born with Blue carries the Pursuer cycle as their fixed identity. Fast rise, cup, restart or crash — this is not occasional behavior, it is the fundamental pattern of their life arc. Curiosity is the engine. The cycle is the teacher.

Window Rhythm
Blue window positions map directly to cycle phases. Sub-Prime is the rise or fall. Variance is the landing — usually a fall, sometimes a cup catch. Stable Swing is the reset point where the next cycle begins or concludes.
