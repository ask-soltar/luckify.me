Yellow Rhythm

Extreme Energy Score +3 to −2 Naval → Solar Plexus Source: Trinity Collective System True Neutral at 0
The Phoenix
Yellow is the berserker rhythm — an extreme approach to everything it touches. It does not operate on a moderate register. It detonates. The impact can be transformative or destructive depending on which polarity is active, but the magnitude is never small. Yellow can manifest physically, mentally, or emotionally — and its extremity is not always visible. Strict, controlled behavior can be Yellow in disguise. The Phoenix identity names the core arc: destruction and rebirth are the same motion. Yellow doesn't recover from its extremes — it becomes through them.
0
Yellow holds the system's true neutral at score 0. This is not merely "no advantage" — it is the system's baseline state before any energetic filter is applied. All other scores are deviations from this point. Outside of the 0 sub-window, Yellow's No Tax sub-windows reduce the usual energetic filtering, allowing whatever is already in motion to express more purely and freely. This is why Yellow windows can feel extreme — not because energy is high, but because the dampening is absent.
Two-Layer Architecture
Birth Rhythm
A person born with Yellow carries extreme energy as their fixed identity. Polarity is always present — the question is which direction is active and whether it is regulated. The Phoenix arc is their life pattern: extremity, consequence, transformation, and return.

Window Rhythm
A Yellow window (score +3 to −2) activates reduced filtering environmentally. The day favors bold action, unfiltered expression, and pure momentum — but polarity is live. What you bring into the window expresses without dampening.
