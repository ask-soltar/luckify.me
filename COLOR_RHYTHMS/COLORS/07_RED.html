Red Rhythm

The Catalyst Score −16 to −29 Palm · Feet · Root Chakras Source: Andromedian Database Authority Required
The Catalyst
Red is the weapon rhythm — dynamic, forceful, and direct. Its nature is rapid onset and equally fast dissipation. A Red firing launches into both the personal and environmental field, leaving a residue bump that shifts the baseline — up or down — regardless of the direction of the original impact. A negative provocation can produce a positive field shift. A positive firing can destabilize. The individual impact looks small. But Red accumulates. Each firing shifts the ambient baseline slightly, and repeated firings compound that shift until the environment reaches a threshold — and detonates. The bomb is not the strike. It is the accumulation.
⚠
Authority Requirement: Without the use of proper authority, Red acts as an energy drain rather than a catalyst. The same force that launches outward turns inward and depletes. Red requires conscious alignment with its own authority to fire constructively. This is the central regulatory challenge of the Red birth rhythm — not suppressing the energy, but ensuring it fires with authorization rather than reactively.
Two-Layer Architecture
Birth Rhythm
A person born with Red carries catalytic weapon energy as their fixed identity. Their presence shifts fields. Their impacts accumulate in environments. Authority management is the core life practice — Red without authority drains; Red with authority catalyzes.

Window Rhythm
A Red window (score −16 to −29) activates catalytic conditions environmentally. Unstable Swing is the category name — the longest band in the system at 14 degrees. The day carries accumulated field pressure. Actions have outsized residue effects. Proceed with authority or not at all.
* Widest single window band in the system — 14 degrees of Unstable Swing
Wave Pattern
Red energy cycle — vertical impacts · residue accumulation · threshold detonation
Vertical impacts — no arc, no curve. Red strikes and dissipates immediately.
Residue accumulates — each impact shifts the baseline. Compound over time.
Threshold detonation — the bomb is the accumulation, not the individual strike.
