04 Guidance Weave Logic
How the three layers combine into a daily output
Every daily guidance output is the intersection of three data points: Color Energy (what rhythm is active), Window Directive (what behavioral position the score calls for), and Category Focus (how that energy expresses through Poker or Daily Life). The Color Energy sets the tone — it's the underlying feel of the day. The Window Directive narrows to the specific score band within that color. The Category Focus translates both into actionable language for the subscriber's chosen lens.
Daily Output = Color Energy + Window Directive + Category Lens
Format: Sentence 1 — name the energy  ·  Sentence 2 — category action  ·  Sentence 3 — precision note or key avoid
Tone rules by color
Pink / Orange / Blue (positive windows) — Lead with what's available. Action-forward. Confident but not reckless.

Yellow (neutral band) — Precision-first. No hype, no alarm. Name the exact condition — Edge, Neutral, Null Edge — and give the appropriate sized response.

Green / Purple — Slower. More interior. Green = presence and attunement. Purple = identity-aware, momentum-dependent. Never catastrophize these windows.

Red — Direct and honest about force. Don't soften it. Name the accumulation dynamic. Authority is the key variable.

Brown — Atmospheric. Elemental. Never analytical about it — Brown is not something to optimize, only to navigate.
Modular category expansion (future)
The two-category system (Poker / Daily Life) is designed to be modular. When additional categories are added — Business, Relationships, Health, etc. — each receives its own guidance column in Section 3, following the same 2–3 sentence format. The Color Identity and Window Directive layers in Sections 1 and 2 remain unchanged; only the category translation layer expands. Subscribers will select one primary focus at signup with the option to switch.
05 Brown / Soltar — Environmental Overlay
Active on all windows when Brown score is present
Brown is not a window that replaces the active color — it is an overlay that modifies any window when active. If a subscriber's day scores −30 to −40, Brown is present regardless of which human rhythm color is primary. The Luck window language is added as a second layer on top of the standard window guidance. Soltar is the voice of this layer in the Luckify product — weekly field intelligence reports are Soltar's expression of Earth energy active in the current period.

When Brown is active: reduce personal pressure framing, increase elemental and environmental language, and always close with a note about staying open rather than optimizing.
Example — Brown active on a Prime (Orange) day · Poker
"The creative floor is on and Orange is protecting your structural edge — engaged play is supported. At the same time, Earth energy is moving today, which means some outcomes will land outside your control in either direction. Play your full game and stay loose with the results; what happens today is partly yours, partly the table's, and partly something larger."
