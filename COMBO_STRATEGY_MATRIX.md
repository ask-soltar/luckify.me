## STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

### Why Element Dominates (43.1%)
- Wu Xing is the **philosophical backbone** of feng shui + color theory
- Element captures the entire chi-flow system in one archetype
- Color → Element is a direct translation (Red=Fire, Green=Wood, etc.)

### Why Exec+Upside Underperforms Element (30.2% vs 43.1%)
- Exec+Upside is **player-centric** (what YOU bring), not environmental
- Element is **venue-centric** (what the course/day brings)
- Golf is heavily influenced by environmental timing (weather, field strength, course setup)

### Why Moon2B Beats Moon10Tithi (31.9% vs 26.4%)
- 2-bucket (Shukla/Krishna) reduces noise
- 10-phase tithi creates fragmentation (too many subcategories with small N)
- Simpler lunar models transfer better than granular ones

### Why Life Path Underperforms Lunar Systems (25.0% vs 31.9%)
- Life Path is **static** (never changes for a player)
- Lunar systems are **dynamic** (change every round)
- Dynamic conditions matter more than static identity for situational performance prediction

### Optimal Combo Formula
**Static Identity Layer × Day-Moving Environment Layer**

✓ Color (dynamic) × Element (static identity of the chi) = 43.1%
✓ Exec+Upside (dynamic player signal) × Moon2B (lunar timing) = 31.9%
✗ Exec+Upside (dynamic player signal) × Life Path (static identity) = 25.0% ← Wrong pairing type
