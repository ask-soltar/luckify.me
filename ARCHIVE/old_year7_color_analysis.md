## Executive Summary

Personal Year 7 players show **consistent positive performance across all major colors**, with win rates ranging from **52.6% to 57.7%** (well above the 50% baseline). This indicates that Year 7 is a universally strong personal year for golf performance, with color (Rhythm) acting as a secondary modifier rather than a primary differentiator.

**Key Finding:** No single color dominates. Instead, Year 7 performers cluster in a tight 5% band (52.6%–57.7% WR), suggesting Year 7 luck transcends color variations.
