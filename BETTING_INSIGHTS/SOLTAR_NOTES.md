## Insight #1: Orange is an Amplifier of Talent (Not Luck)

**Math Finding:**
- Orange × Exec 75-100 × Earth: −1.242 vs_avg (beats field by 1.24 strokes)
- Orange × Exec 50-75 × Open: +0.461 vs_avg (loses to field by 0.46 strokes)
- Orange × Exec 25-50 × Fire: +0.307 vs_avg (loses to field by 0.31 strokes)

**Pattern:** Orange's effect is **proportional to Exec level**. High skill + Orange = maximum outperformance. Low skill + Orange = maximum underperformance.

### Soltar's Insight

**Orange's archetype:** "Protective Creative Energy — steady, body-level, and safe."

**Why it amplifies talent:**
- Orange is **grounded in reality** (body-level, earth-connected)
- No escape routes, no illusions, no luck buffer
- Player's actual skill *shows up* under Orange's steady state
- Earth element (grounded) amplifies this: "what you do is what you get"

**Why the market prices it correctly:**
- Talent-based signals are **actually priceable**
- Books can measure Exec levels and condition changes
- Orange + Earth is **repeatable, not random**
- High-Exec Orange players *consistently* beat the field
- Low-Exec Orange players *consistently* lose

**Market implication:**
Orange signals are **skill-based, not informational.** The book knows a low-skill player will lose in Orange. They adjust odds accordingly. This reduces our edge because skill is already public information.

### Betting Application

**Do NOT chase Orange × High Exec × Earth as a primary edge** — the market already knows high-skill players outperform in grounded conditions.

**DO hunt Orange × Exec mismatches:**
- Look for **low-skill players getting good odds** in Orange + Earth (market underweighting skill penalty)
- Look for **market overvaluing Orange players** based on recent results (if those results were in lucky conditions, Orange will regress in unlucky conditions)

This is the core difference between Orange and other colors:
- **Pink/Blue:** Energetic potential, luck-dependent, high variance
- **Red:** Catalytic force, requires authority alignment
- **Green:** Growth rhythm, needs directional alignment
- **Orange:** Foundational skill amplifier — talent shows, weakness shows, no buffer

Orange archetype: "The Metaphysical Shield" — protective, steady, body-level, safe.

**In operational reality:**
- High Exec Orange player = **transparent skill advantage** (no luck needed)
- Low Exec Orange player = **transparent disadvantage** (no luck to save them)
- Medium Exec Orange player = **medium performance** (exactly what you'd expect)

This is why Orange signals are **priceable by books**. Talent is public knowledge. The market knows what Orange does: amplifies your true ability.

### Betting Implications

**Orange signals are skill-based, not informational.**

**Betting directive:** Prioritize Orange rhythm players *in grounded conditions* (Calm, Earth element, structured rounds) because:
